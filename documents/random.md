# Random Space Facts

## Venus Has Slow Days
A single day on Venus lasts about 243 Earth days because the planet spins extremely slowly in the opposite direction of most planets.

## Neutron Star Density
Neutron stars are so dense that a teaspoon of their material would weigh roughly as much as Mount Everest.

## Cosmic Water Reservoir
There is a water vapor cloud in the constellation Aquila that holds enough water to supply all of Earth’s oceans for billions of years.

## Coldest Known Place
In the Boomerang Nebula, temperatures dip to about 1 Kelvin, making it the coldest known natural place in the observable universe.

## Titan’s Methane Lakes
Saturn’s moon Titan has lakes and rivers of liquid methane and ethane, complete with rainfall in a hydrocarbon weather cycle.
